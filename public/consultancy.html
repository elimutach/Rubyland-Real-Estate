Consultation FAQs

Booking & Preparation

How do I prepare for my consultation?

We recommend: 1) Gather any property documents you have, 2) Define your investment goals and budget, 3) Prepare a list of specific questions, 4) Have your identification documents ready, 5) Think about your preferred timeline for investment or decision-making.

What is your cancellation/rescheduling policy?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. You can reschedule through the confirmation email link or by calling our office. Late cancellations (less than 24 hours) may incur a fee of 50% of the consultation cost.

Do you offer emergency consultation slots?

Yes, we offer emergency consultation slots for time-sensitive property decisions. These are subject to consultant availability and may incur a 20% premium. Contact our emergency line at +254 720 000 000 for immediate assistance.

Can I bring someone with me to the consultation?

Absolutely! We encourage bringing partners, family members, or business associates who are involved in the decision-making process. Please let us know in advance if more than 2 additional people will be attending.

What happens after I book my consultation?

You'll receive: 1) Confirmation email with date/time details, 2) Pre-consultation questionnaire (optional), 3) Meeting link or location details, 4) Consultant profile, 5) List of documents to bring. We'll also send a reminder 24 hours before your appointment.

Consultation Process

What is included in a paid consultation?

Our paid consultations include: 1) One-on-one session with an expert consultant, 2) Customized property analysis, 3) Market insights relevant to your needs, 4) Actionable recommendations, 5) Follow-up summary email, 6) 30-day email support for clarification questions.

How are consultants assigned to clients?

We match you with a consultant based on: 1) Your specific consultation topic, 2) Geographic location of your property interest, 3) Consultant specialization and expertise, 4) Language preferences, 5) Your preferred consultation format (in-person/virtual).

Do you provide consultation reports or documentation?

Yes, all paid consultations include a summary report emailed within 48 hours. Comprehensive written reports with detailed analysis are available as an add-on service. Sample reports are available upon request before booking.

Can I record the consultation session?

Audio recording of virtual consultations is permitted with prior notification to your consultant. Video recording requires written consent. All recordings are for personal use only and may not be distributed or published. We do not provide recording services.

What if I need follow-up consultations?

Follow-up consultations are available at a discounted rate (20% off standard fee). Package deals for multiple sessions are also offered. Your initial consultant will remain your primary contact for continuity, unless you request a specialist in a different area.
