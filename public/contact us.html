Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

How quickly will I get a response after submitting the contact form?

We guarantee a response within 2 business hours during our working hours (Monday-Friday, 8 AM-5 PM). For inquiries submitted outside these hours, you'll receive a response by the next business day morning.

What information should I provide when contacting about property valuation?

Please provide: Property location (full address), size (acres or square feet), current use, any existing structures, title deed details, and the purpose of valuation (sale, mortgage, insurance, etc.). Photos and survey maps are helpful if available.

Do you offer free initial consultations?

Yes, we offer a free 30-minute initial consultation for new clients. This allows us to understand your needs and provide preliminary guidance on how we can assist you with your real estate requirements.

What areas in Kenya do you cover?

We operate nationwide with offices in Nairobi, Mombasa, and Kisumu. Our services extend to all counties in Kenya, including remote areas where we have established partnerships with local professionals.

How do I verify that my property title is genuine?

Our title verification process includes: Cross-checking with land registry records, verifying ownership history, checking for any encumbrances or charges, and confirming survey details. We provide a comprehensive report with our findings.

Services & Processes

How long does a full property valuation take?

A complete property valuation typically takes 3-5 business days from site visit to report delivery. Complex properties or those requiring additional research may take up to 7 business days.

What's included in your feasibility studies?

Our feasibility studies include: Market analysis, financial projections, risk assessment, regulatory compliance check, site suitability analysis, and detailed recommendations with implementation timeline.

Can you help with international property transactions?

Yes, we assist international clients with Kenyan property transactions, including remote due diligence, virtual tours, legal coordination, and liaison with local authorities on your behalf.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, mobile money (M-Pesa, Airtel Money), credit/debit cards, and checks. International clients can pay via wire transfer or through our secure online payment portal.

Do you offer emergency property consultation?

Yes, we offer emergency consultation services for urgent situations like fraudulent property deals, imminent transactions, or legal deadlines. Contact our emergency line: +254 720 000 000.
