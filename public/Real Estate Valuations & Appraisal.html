Professional Real Estate Valuation Services

Valuation is the independent process of determining the market value of land, buildings, and other property interests for financing, insurance, taxation, litigation, or transaction purposes.

At Rubyland Limited, we combine 24+ years of local market expertise with internationally recognized valuation methodologies to deliver accurate, defensible property valuations.

Our valuation reports are trusted by banks, investors, insurance companies, and government agencies throughout Kenya and the wider East African region.
