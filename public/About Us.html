Our Legacy of Excellence

24+ Years of Transforming Real Estate in Kenya

Founded in 1999, Rubyland Limited emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to bring transparency, expertise, and trust to Kenya's real estate market. What began as a small valuation firm has grown into East Africa's premier real estate consultancy.

We've witnessed the market's evolution, navigated economic shifts, and consistently delivered results that protect and grow our clients' investments. Our journey is marked by thousands of successful transactions, lasting client relationships, and an unwavering commitment to ethical practice.

Today, with offices across Kenya and expanding regional presence, we continue to set the standard for real estate excellence, guided by our founding principles of integrity, expertise, and client-first service.
