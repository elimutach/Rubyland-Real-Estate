Proper management of a property will produce the desired investment objectives. Property management goes beyond collecting and remitting rent to the landlord. The ability to do so timely and with ease while handling tenants with respect and dignity is crucial.

At Rubyland Limited, property management is not a job but a relationship that is built over time to benefit all parties. We do so while ensuring the property is kept clean and in good condition in terms of repairs and maintenance.

Our Due Diligence Process

Step 1

Registration & Licensing

By Law, all persons practicing as Property Managers or Agents in Kenya must be Registered and Licensed by the Estate Agents Registration Board and have a current Professional Indemnity Cover to protect members of the public. Rubyland is duly compliant and can be trusted to safeguard your property.

Step 2

Legal Expertise

Rubyland has been involved in the management of all types of properties commercial and residential with an experience of more than 24 years. We are well equipped to handle all the legal intricacies involving property management including protected and controlled tenancies as well as levying distress for non payment.

Step 3

Investment Vehicle Management

As a company, we go beyond rent collection. We have the experience and skills to take care of your property as an investment vehicle by managing the stakeholders effectively be they the landlord, tenants, service providers, government agencies etc., while making sure the property is well maintained.

Step 4

Risk Management

As property managers we ensure the economic life of the property is safeguarded. We ensure service costs are managed effectively not to affect rent while taking care of tenants concerns. We manage concerns before they develop into conflicts thus minimizing voids which affect property returns.
